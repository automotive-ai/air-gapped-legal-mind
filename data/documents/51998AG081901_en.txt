INTRODUCTION 1. On 2 December 1996 the Commission forwarded a proposal, based on article 100a of the EC Treaty, relating to motor vehicles and their trailers intended for the transport of dangerous goods by road and amending Directive 70/156/EEC relating to the type-approval of motor vehicles and their trailers.2. The European Parliament delivered its Opinion on 19 February 1998 proposing one amendment.3. The Economic and Social Committee delivered its Opinion on 9 July 1997.4. On 29 June 1998, the Council adopted a common position on the Commission proposal in accordance with Article 189b of the Treaty.II. OBJECTIVE 5. The objective of the proposal for a directive is to establish the technical requirements for motor vehicles and their trailers intended for the transport of dangerous goods by road and to ensure total harmonisation of the relevant technical provisions.III. ANALYSIS OF THE COMMON POSITION 6. Enacting termsThe articles of the common position have been revised in order to bring them into line with the structure of other recent Directives adopted by the Council and the European Parliament in the motor vehicles field (cf. Directive 97/27/EC relating to masses and dimensions of certain categories of motor vehicles).In Article 2 the definition of the ADR agreement has been deleted since there is no reference to that agreement in the remaining articles or in the annexes. A reference has been included, however, in the preamble in order to demonstrate the link between this Directive and the said agreement.7. AnnexesThe amendments of the technical annexes are mainly editorial adjustments to take account on the one hand of the enlargement of the scope introduced in Annex I, 1.1, and on the other hand of Directive 97/27/EC which has been adopted since the Commission put forward its proposal. The Council has simplified the definition in Annex I, 2.1, by cross referring to the technical provisions relevant to the transport of dangerous goods contained in Directive 94/55/EC, Annex lB, as the aspects listed in the Commission proposal were judged unnecessarily restrictive on manufacturers.Annex II has been aligned on the standard administrative type-approval provisions flowing from Directive 92/53/EEC.Appendix 1 to Annex II has also been aligned on Directives 92/53/EEC and 97/27/EC. Furthermore, more detailed sub-items have been added to section 8 ('Brakes`), and two new sections, 12 ('Miscellaneous`) and 14 ('Special provisions for vehicles intended for the transport of dangerous goods`) have been introduced. Section 2 relating to masses and dimensions has been removed since it is not relevant for the type-approval purposes of vehicles intended for the carriage of dangerous goods.
